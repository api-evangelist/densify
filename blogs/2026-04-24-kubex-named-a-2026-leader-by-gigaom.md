Visibility Was Never the Hard Part Industry analyst recognition means something different from an award. GigaOm does not hand out trophies. They evaluate products against a defined capability framework and tell the market where vendors actually stand. By that measure, Kubex has been named a Leader in two of GigaOm’s 2026 Radar Reports: Kubernetes Resource Management and Cloud Resource Optimization. In the Kubernetes report, we are positioned as an Outperformer. In Cloud Resource Optimization, a Fast Mover. Both place Kubex in the Innovation/Platform Play quadrant.
